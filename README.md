# Anis – Your Apartment Life Organizer

Anis is a smart, user-friendly mobile app designed to make apartment-sharing life smooth and stress-free. It manages roommates, finances, chores, schedules, and shared agreements in one place, ensuring transparency, fairness, and accountability. It’s built to evolve gradually, starting with core features and expanding over time.

## Core Features

### 1. Roommate Management
- Create and manage profiles for all roommates.
- Track contact info and basic details.
- Assign roles and responsibilities (starting with “roommate”).

### 2. Money & Expense Tracking
- Track rent, utilities, and shared expenses.
- Allow fixed or percentage-based rent splits.
- Record who paid, who owes, and due dates.
- Maintain a complete history/log of all transactions.
- Show balances in real-time and notify users of debts.

### 3. Chores & Household Tasks
- Assign chores to specific roommates.
- Track completion and streaks for accountability.
- Optional system for punishments or rewards.
- Calendar integration for chore schedules.

### 4. Shared Calendar & Scheduling
- Display personal and shared events.
- Set reminders for rent, bills, chores, or gatherings.
- Provide clear visual overview of household activities.

### 5. Notes & Agreements
- Space for house rules, agreements, or shared notes.
- Versioning or logs to track changes and maintain memory.

### 6. Multi-language Support
- Fully functional in English and French.
- All notifications, interface elements, and logs support both languages.

## Advanced Features (Future Updates)
- **Analytics & Reporting:** Monthly summaries of expenses, chore completion, and other household metrics.
- **Payment Integration:** Link to bank or payment apps to track transactions automatically.
- **Gamification:** Badges, rewards, or leaderboards for chore completion and positive behavior.
- **Notifications:** Alerts via app, SMS, or email for payments, chores, and events.
- **Backup & Restore:** Securely save and recover data.

## Design Principles
- **Transparency:** Everyone can see shared finances and responsibilities.
- **Simplicity:** Easy to navigate, minimal friction for everyday use.
- **Accountability:** Tracks chores and payments to prevent misunderstandings.
- **Flexibility:** Supports different apartment arrangements, roommate agreements, and languages.

## End Goal
Anis becomes a single hub for apartment-sharing life. It handles everything from finances to chores, ensures clear communication, and reduces conflicts. Roommates never have to guess who owes what or when chores are due—everything is logged, visible, and actionable.
